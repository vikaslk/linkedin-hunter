## RED FLAGS TO AVOID IN JOB POSTINGS

⚠️ **Skip These:**
- "Must have 15+ years in specific subspecialty" (too narrow)
- Contract or temporary positions (you want full-time + equity)
- Pure clinical roles with no strategy component
- Medical writing, medical coding, pharma sales
- Delhi/Mumbai locations (not on your list)
- Remote-only positions (want to relocate)
- Companies with very poor Glassdoor reviews
- Unpaid "advisor" roles at early startups

✅ **Look For These:**
- Mentions of equity/stock options
- Series A-C funding stage
- "Build from ground up" or "Scale operations"
- Combining clinical + business
- Target geographies mentioned
- Recent funding announcements
- Growing team (not replacement hire)
- Clear reporting structure to CEO/founders

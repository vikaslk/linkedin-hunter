## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Issue: Comet returns "No results found"**
- Increase date range to 14 or 21 days
- Remove some geography filters
- Simplify search strings (use fewer Boolean operators)

**Issue: Too many irrelevant results**
- Tighten CRITICAL EXCLUSIONS
- Add specific company size filters
- Focus on 2-3 geographies only

**Issue: Comet times out or stops mid-search**
- Run Phase 1 separately first
- Then run Phase 2+3 with Phase 1 results
- Or reduce from 48 search combinations to 24 (focus on top 2 geographies)

**Issue: LinkedIn URLs don't work**
- Copy URL and paste in incognito browser
- Or search company + role title manually on LinkedIn
- This happens when jobs are very recent or region-locked
